{% blocktrans trimmed %} YourNextMP Jamaica is a community-run initiative aimed at pulling together information on all candidates seeking office in the 2016 General election in one place, giving you a full picture of your candidates and your constituency. It is made possible by volunteer contributions and hosting by the SlashRoots Foundation in collaboration with mySociety. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Digital Technologies, such as the Internet and Mobile phones are transforming our society. We believe technology can facilitate new channels of participation for the public and better inform us in our decisions throughout the electoral process. This is website is intended to demonstrate that possibility. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} The official list of candidates is available on the official Electoral Office Of Jamaica website as a PDF document. The records of the candidates on this site are sourced by volunteers like yourself. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} If you have any questions on the initiative, please email {{ email }} with any feedback or bug reports. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} The source code for this site is available on GitHub, and you can find a list of known bugs there as well. {% endblocktrans %}
