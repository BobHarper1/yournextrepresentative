Report results

Anyone can add a result, which will go into our unverified database.

Results with official sources are likely to help us most of all.

A team of people at LGiU and Democracy Club will work through these suggested results to verify them.

At the moment, we're only covering local elections. If you know about other results, please tweet us

1. Council Control

Has a council announced the controlling party? Tell us about it!

  1. Find a source URL for the information
  2. Navigate to the council's page on our site
  3. Press 'Report council control', enter the party and your source
  4. Go and hunt for more results!

2. Votes cast

Who won? Can you enter the votes cast per person?

  1. Find a source URL for the information
  2. Navigate to the council's page on our site
  3. Find the ward you have results for
  4. Press 'Report votes'
  5. Enter the results, checking them carefully. Please enter a good source for this information
  6. Go and hunt for more results!
