Please email yournextmp@mysociety.org with any feedback or bug reports. If you'd like to discuss this site or any other project relating to elections in the UK, we'd suggest that you post to the Democracy Club Google Group.

The source code for this site is available on GitHub, and you can find a list of known bugs there as well.

This site was developed by Mark Longair (mySociety), Sym Roe (Democracy Club), Jenny Duckett and Zarino Zappia (mySociety).

The data on candidates from the last UK general election in 2010 is all taken from Edmund von der Burg's YourNextMP.com, with many thanks.

You can use the data from this site, via the API or CSV download, under the terms of the Creative Commons license Attribution-ShareAlike (CC-BY-SA) with the exception of:

  • The party logos, which are taken from the Electoral Commission's website
  • and candidate photos. The candidate photos are typically either submitted by the candidates themselves, or from sources where it seems reasonable that we might use them on this site, such as the party's official page for that candidate.

By submitting data to this site, you agree that it may be distributed from this site under CC-BY-SA or (in case we decide to offer it under alternative licenses after further discussion) CC0 or ODbL.
